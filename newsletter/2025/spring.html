Did You Know?

The biological samples and information that participants share through Connect serve as valuable snapshots for future research, capturing data as health changes occur over time. Unlike the samples collected during routine clinical health care, which are used to address immediate health questions, these research samples are stored and will offer unique insights into future health questions specifically for long-term study.
