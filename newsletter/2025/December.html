Connect Participant's Son is Her "Why" for Joining

Eileen from Sanford Health is passionate about doing all she can to ensure her family has a healthy future. Marshfield Hosts Participant Event

This past fall, Connect researchers at the National Cancer Institute joined the Marshfield Clinic team to host a research chat for local Connect participants. The event brought together participants and research team members for a conversation about Connect's goals, the future of the study, and ideas for continuing to engage participants.
